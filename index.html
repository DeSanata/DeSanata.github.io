About us

About AFRO HAIR STYLE

We are a small business that started one year ago. This idea of opening a salon had been in my head since I was young, I appreciated the fact of seeing people with beautiful hairstyles and I liked having my hair done myself but on the other hand I appreciated less the time we spent detangling hair because of the quirks that Afro hair can have regardless of its type

That's why I wanted to open this salon which specializes in Afro hair whatever the type from the thinnest to the hardest to the longest to the shorter ones. It is our responsibility to do everything possible to keep your hair beautiful and healthy
